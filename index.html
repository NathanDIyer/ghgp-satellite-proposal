What this means for GHGP Scope 2
1

It works now, with information you already have

Location and annual MWh are already in every certificate registry. Add offshore/onshore and you have an hourly shape accurate to ±2 pp (offshore) or ±3 pp (onshore). No new data collection needed.

2

3 months of real data drops error to ~1 pp

A short calibration period from any time window—calibrate once, use forever. The parameters persist indefinitely because ERA5 wind patterns are stable year to year.

3

The default is conservative, by design

Satellite shapes slightly undercount hourly alignment, so no one is overcredited. Generators who invest in metering will always score higher—a natural incentive to get real data without excluding anyone.

4

It works everywhere in the world

ERA5 is global and free. Validated across the UK, Canada, Australia, Belgium, Denmark, and New Zealand. Every wind farm on Earth can participate in hourly matching today.
